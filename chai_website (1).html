Our Mission

The Coastal Homeless Action Initiative (CHAI) is dedicated to uplifting the homeless community in Corpus Christi through collaboration and service.

By partnering with local pharmacies and supporting youth volunteers, CHAI helps strengthen homeless shelters across the city, providing consistent resources and hands-on assistance.

Our mission is to build a network of care that empowers young people to make a difference while ensuring those without stable housing receive the dignity and support they deserve.

Support Our Cause

Our Impact

Community Partnerships: We collaborate with local pharmacies to ensure consistent supply of essential resources to homeless shelters.

Youth Engagement: We empower young volunteers to make meaningful contributions to their community while developing leadership skills.

Shelter Support: Through hands-on assistance and resource provision, we help strengthen homeless shelters across Corpus Christi.

Network Building: We create connections between community members, organizations, and those in need to build a stronger support system.

Our Vision

Through compassion, action, and community partnerships, CHAI works toward creating a stronger, more connected Corpus Christi.

We believe that by bringing together young volunteers, local businesses, and community organizations, we can create lasting change in addressing homelessness in our city.

Every person deserves dignity, support, and the opportunity to rebuild their life with the help of a caring community.

About CHAI

The Coastal Homeless Action Initiative is a youth-led organization dedicated to addressing homelessness in Corpus Christi through innovative partnerships and community service.

We believe in the power of young people to create meaningful change in their communities.
